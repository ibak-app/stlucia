Practical Tools & Information

▼
Quick reference for currency conversion, time zone, dialing code, driving rules, visa requirements, and emergency numbers.

Currency Converter

XCD is pegged at 2.70 to 1 USD. Quick conversion: multiply USD by 2.70 for XCD, divide XCD by 2.70 for USD.

Time Zone

Atlantic Standard Time (AST), UTC-4. No daylight saving time. Same as US Eastern during summer, 1 hour ahead in winter.

Dialing Code

Country code: +1-758. St. Lucia is part of the North American Numbering Plan. Dial 1-758-XXX-XXXX from the US/Canada.

Driving

Drive on the LEFT (UK style). Temporary driving permit required for visitors (available from car rental companies or police station). Valid for 3 months.

Visa Requirements

Citizens of many countries (US, UK, Canada, EU, CARICOM) do not need a visa for visits up to 6 weeks. Check the Immigration Department for specific requirements.

Emergency Numbers

Police: 999 | Fire: 911 | Ambulance: 911 | Coast Guard: 758-452-2656
